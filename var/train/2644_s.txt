The	agreement	resolved	a	dispute	that	arose	in	June	when	Colombia	turned	down	American	's	request	to	operate	flights	between	New	York	and	Bogota	,	a	denial	that	prompted	the	United	States	to	charge	that	the	Colombians	were	breaking	a	bilateral	aviation	agreement	and	to	propose	sanctions	against	one	of	two	Colombian	airlines	,	Avianca	and	ACES	.
